Bowl of Nouns

How to play

  1. Gather a group of friends (minimum of 6)
  2. Fill the bowl with nouns. Be as creative, clever, and silly as possible
  3. A player from team 1 starts with the bowl, and is given one minute. The objective is to have your team guess the nouns in order to have them removed from the bowl
    (1 noun = 1 point)
  4. Once his/her minute is up it goes to a player from Team 2 and they are given one minute to do the same
  5. The bowl is then passed to a new player from Team 1 and the pattern continues
  6. A round ends when every noun is removed from the bowl. The nouns are then placed back into the bowl to begin the next round

Overview

The game is composed of 3 rounds...

  • WORD SPLASH: DESCRIBE the noun to YOUR TEAM without explicitly stating what is written
  • MUTE MAYHEM: ACT the noun to YOUR TEAM without speaking or making any sounds
  • ONE WORD: STATE one word associated with the noun to prompt YOUR TEAM to guess the noun. Once the word is stated, you can only repeat that word, but you may vary inflection
